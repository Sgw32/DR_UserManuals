%% Abstract (recommended length around 80-200 words)
\newcommand*{\ManualAbstract}{%
    This user manual documents the Digifiz Replica family of digital dashboards developed for Volkswagen Golf Mk2, Jetta Mk2, and Scirocco Mk2 vehicles.
    It explains how the Replica and Replica Next instrument clusters replace the factory panel, documents the model coding scheme, and summarizes the supplied hardware.
    The guide details the electrical interfaces, connector pin-outs, operating principle, and measurement capabilities, and provides the environmental limits required for reliable use.
    Step-by-step instructions cover removal of the factory cluster, installation of the Replica hardware, day-to-day operation, and the multi-function (MFA) features.
    Dedicated chapters describe configuration of the Wi-Fi enabled Replica Next firmware, Bluetooth configuration for the classic Replica, and extensive troubleshooting advice for typical issues.
    Final sections summarize labelling, packaging, and storage recommendations and provide a change log for the manual.
}
