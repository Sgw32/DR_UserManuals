%% Abstract (recommended length around 80-200 words)
\newcommand*{\ManualAbstract}{%
    This user manual documents the \ReplicaGenOne{} and \ReplicaNextLong{} families of digital dashboards developed for Volkswagen Golf Mk2, Jetta Mk2, and Scirocco Mk2 vehicles.
    It explains how the \ReplicaGenOneShort{} and \ReplicaNextShort{} instrument clusters replace the factory panel, documents the model coding scheme, and summarizes the supplied hardware.
    The guide details the electrical interfaces, connector pin-outs, operating principle, and measurement capabilities, and provides the environmental limits required for reliable use.
    Step-by-step instructions cover removal of the factory cluster, installation of the Replica hardware, day-to-day operation, and the multi-function (MFA) features.
    Dedicated chapters describe configuration of the Wi-Fi enabled \ReplicaNextPlain{} firmware, Bluetooth configuration for the classic \ReplicaGenOneShort{}, and extensive troubleshooting advice for typical issues.
    Final sections summarize labelling, packaging, and storage recommendations and provide a change log for the manual.
}
